How do I make a booking on your website?

Two way to make booking on our website via our website you can book now taxi also you can call us to book a taxi.

What are the services offered by Intercity riders?

One Way Travel cab offers a reliable one way taxi service in india through a fleet of modern, air conditioned and Non- air conditioned taxi. The service is backed by a 24x7 Customer Experience Center.

What are the cancellation charges?

If you cancel your journey within 24 hours or there is a no-show, you are not entitled to any cancellation charge. But in case you cancel your booking more than 24 hours ahead of your journey, you are entitled to a full refund.

Can I make a change in my booking at a later stage?

You need to call up on Yatras helpline number for the same. However, your pickup address cannot be changed and any further changes on your booking will be subject to availability of the car type on your journey date. In case there is an additional charge in the process, it will have to be incurred by the customer.

What are the timings for the Intercity Service?

One Way Travel Cab Services are available 24 hrs a day, 365 days a year..
